Places to go in Wellington

Here you can find information about Wellington and all it has to offer!

First up there's Te Papa!

Te Papa has 36,000 square metres of public exhibitions, with six levels of history.

Then we have Weta Workshop!

Weta workshop do daily tours where you can see the incredible work that their artists have created for various international film productions.

Including The Lord Of The Rings, Avatar and the Hobbit.


Finally we have Zelandia!

Zelandia is a bird sanctuary, where you can see native and international birdlife.
