Remarkable Restaurants in Wellington

Hello! Here are some places to go in Wellington for delicious food!

Jarden Grill

Jardin Grill is a good place to go for meat lovers, Jardin Grill restaurant can be found in the Sofitel.

Logan Brown

For a more fine dining experience head to Logan Brown restaurant.

The restaurant is situated close to the waterfront and has it has vegetarian, vegan and gluten free options. The average meal price is $70-$95.

Charley Noble

A budget friendly restaurant is Charley Noble. It is found by the waterfront, and it is vegetarian friendly.
